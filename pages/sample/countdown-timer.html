Countdown timer

The countdown timer component helps visitors visualize the counting toward or away from a fixed point in time.

Time intervals displayed by the countdown timer can include:

  • Weeks
  • Days
  • Hours
  • Minutes
  • Seconds

Usage

When and how to use it

Use the countdown timer to emphasize important events. These events might include:

  • Release dates
  • Application openings and/or deadlines
  • The launch of new products or services

How not to use it

Try not to use months in your countdown as they present a fluctuating metric of time.

Avoid excessive timeframes (more than 1 year) as this renders the countdown useless to visitors.
