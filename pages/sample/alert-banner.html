Alert Banner

You can now add an alert banner at the top of your website for communicating important information which is meant to alert users. The Alert banner can be closed by the user using the x button in the top right corner.

Usage

Generally an Alert Banner will be added to your page although it can be included on any page. Add the alert key with the path to your alert html file alert: "../modules/alert-banner.html" after your eleventy layout key declaration in the front matter data on your page. 

---
title: Alert Banner
layout: page.njk
alert: "../modules/alert-banner.html"
---

Or include alert banner module into your page directly after skip-to-content component:

<header role="banner" id="header" class="global-header">
<div id="skip-to-content">
    <a href="#main-content">Skip to Main Content</a>
</div>

<!-- Alert Banner -->
❴% include "../modules/alert-banner.html" %❵
...
</header>
