Structure

Our structural components form the basic structure of a website using the State Web Template. This structure contains elements that connect web pages and can lead to a smooth user experience.

Webpage structure

The State Web Template uses includes as our main building components.

Structural elements

Utility header

Places a standard header at the very top of your website.

Site header

Provides space for your siteâ€™s name at the top of your website.

Mobile controls

Mobile Controls are the search and navigation toggle buttons that expand and collapse search and navigation sections in mobile view

Search

Allows visitors to search for specific content using keywords or phrases.

Site navigation

Appears at the top of every page on your website and links to pages in the site.

Footer

Places a standard footer at the very bottom of your website.
