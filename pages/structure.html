Structure

Structural components form the basic structure of a website that uses the State Web Template. This structure contains elements that connect the web pages together.

Webpage structure

There are six main structural components in the State Web Template.

Structural elements

Utility header

Places a standard header at the very top of your website.

Site header

Provides space for your siteâ€™s name at the top of your website.

Mobile controls

Expand and collapse search and navigation sections in mobile view.

Search

Allows visitors to search for specific content using keywords or phrases.

Site navigation

Appears at the top of every page on your website and links to pages in the site.

Footer

Places a standard footer at the very bottom of your website.
