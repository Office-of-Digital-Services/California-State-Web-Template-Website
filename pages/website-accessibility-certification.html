2023 Certification

Agency:

California Department of Technology

Certification date:

June 15, 2023

Contact:

ServiceDesk@state.ca.gov

The undersigned certify that, as of June 5, 2023 the website of the California Department of Technology is designed, developed, and maintained to be accessible. This denotes compliance with the following:

  • California Government Code Sections 7405, 11135, and 11546.7
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1 published by the Web Accessibility Initiative of the World Wide Web Consortium
  • At a minimum Level AA success criteria
